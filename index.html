About us

SJKMS Institute of Engineering

Situated against the stunning backdrop of Nepal, SJKMS Institute of Engineering stands as a catalyst for academic brilliance and forward-thinking education. Committed to shaping the brightest minds, the institute seamlessly blends theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students to navigate the dynamic challenges of engineering. State-of-the-art facilities, including cutting-edge laboratories and modern classrooms, inspire exploration and innovation. The faculty, comprising seasoned educators and industry experts, not only imparts theoretical knowledge but also shares practical insights. SJKMS Institute of Engineering is more than an institution; it is a vibrant community that fuels aspirations and molds the engineers of tomorrow.

DRC ( Disaster Recovery Center )

The Disaster Recovery Center (DRC) is located in Huprachaur, Hetauda and is being operated under National Information Technology Center, Ministry of Communication & Information Technology. The DRC site offers a backup infrastructure for Government Integrated Data Center at Singhadurbar in order to provide business continuity and faster recovery in an event of disaster.
